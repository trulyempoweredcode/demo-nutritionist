Your first consultation is a thorough 75-minute session where we'll go through your full health history, review your diet diary, and discuss your goals in detail. I'll ask about your digestion, energy levels, sleep, stress, and any symptoms you're experiencing. You'll leave with initial dietary recommendations and a clear idea of how we'll work together going forward. It's relaxed, supportive, and entirely focused on you.

Some clients see significant improvements after just a couple of sessions, while others prefer ongoing support over a longer period.
